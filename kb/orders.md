# Orders & Payments

## Order Changes
Orders cannot be modified once they have been processed for shipment.

Customers who need to correct an address should contact support as soon as possible after placing an order.

## Order Cancellations
Orders may be canceled only if they have not yet been shipped.

Once an order has shipped, it is no longer eligible for cancellation.

## Payment Methods
We accept:
- Major credit cards
- Debit cards
- Digital wallets where available

All payments are processed securely.
