# Refunds & Returns Policy

## Eligibility for Returns
Due to the perishable nature of food products, we do not accept returns on opened items.

Unopened items may be eligible for return if:
- The item was damaged during shipping
- The incorrect product was shipped

Return requests must be submitted within 7 days of delivery.

## Refund Process
If a return is approved:
- Customers may be asked to provide photos of the damaged or incorrect item
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method

## Non-Refundable Items
We do not offer refunds for:
- Opened food products
- Items returned without prior approval
- Orders delayed due to customs inspections
