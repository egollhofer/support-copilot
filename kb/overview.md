# Olivetto Imports – Customer Support Overview

Olivetto Imports specializes in premium imported food products, including extra virgin olive oil, vinegars, and specialty pantry items sourced from small producers in Europe.

We ship directly to customers in the United States and Canada. All products are food-grade, properly labeled, and imported in compliance with local regulations.

Customer support is available Monday–Friday, 9am–5pm ET.
Support inquiries are typically answered within 1–2 business days.
