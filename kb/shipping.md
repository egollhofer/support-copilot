# Shipping & Delivery

## Shipping Regions
We currently ship to:
- United States (all 50 states)
- Canada

We do not ship internationally outside North America at this time.

## Processing Time
Orders are processed within 1–2 business days after payment is confirmed.

## Delivery Time
- Standard shipping: 5–7 business days
- Expedited shipping: 2–3 business days

Delivery times may be longer during holidays or due to customs delays for imported goods.

## Shipping Delays
Delays may occur due to:
- Weather conditions
- Carrier disruptions
- Customs inspections

If an order has not arrived within 10 business days of shipment, customers may contact support for assistance.
